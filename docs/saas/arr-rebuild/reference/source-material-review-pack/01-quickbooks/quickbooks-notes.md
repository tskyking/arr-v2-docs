# QuickBooks Notes

## Why useful
QuickBooks references help identify how invoice/customer/item data may be structured or constrained.

## What to look for
- item/product/service naming conventions
- invoice import formatting rules
- category/subcategory formatting
- customer/invoice field patterns
- export/import limits and assumptions

## Notable observation
QuickBooks references often focus more on import mechanics than contract semantics, so they are useful for field-shape testing but less useful for full contract logic.
