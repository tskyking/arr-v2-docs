# Chargebee Notes

## Why useful
Chargebee is helpful as a subscription-billing reference because it exposes export jobs, CSV/ZIP data extraction, and subscription-specific bulk/export patterns.

## What to look for
- subscription export shapes
- billing period exports
- item-level subscription updates
- export job metadata and downloadable ZIP workflows

## Notable observation
Chargebee references are good for understanding recurring subscription data structures, but less useful for full enterprise contract nuance by themselves.
